Our Legal Area

  • Family law

    Family law encompasses legal matters like divorce, child custody, and spousal support, regulating relationships and resolving conflicts within familial structures.

  • Real Estate law

    Real estate law involves legal aspects of property transactions, covering buying, selling, leasing, and addressing disputes between property owners and tenants.

  • Business law

    Business law governs commercial transactions, and corporate operations, addressing legal aspects of business activities, from formation to dissolution.

  • Criminal law

    Criminal law defines and punishes offenses against the state, ensuring justice through legal procedures and penalties for unlawful actions.

  • Finance law

    Finance law regulates financial transactions, institutions, and markets, ensuring compliance with laws that protect investors and maintain financial stability.

  • Divorce law

    Divorce law governs legal dissolution of marriage, addressing issues like asset division, spousal support, and child custody through legal procedures.
